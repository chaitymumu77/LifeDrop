Who We Are

A student-led initiative connecting blood donors with patients across Bangladesh.

Our Mission

LifeDrop is a non-profit, open-source project aimed at eliminating the gap between blood donors and patients. We believe no one should lose their life due to lack of access to safe blood.

Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP/MySQL, our system is designed to be lightweight, fast, and accessible even on slow internet connections.

  • ✅ Create an accessible directory of ready donors across Bangladesh
  • ✅ Spread awareness about blood group shortages in local areas
  • ✅ Use modern technology to make blood requests simple and fast
